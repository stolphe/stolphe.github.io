The funds presented here only help civilians and do not sponsor any side's military equipment. I/WE are for civilians.


Ukrainian Red Cross - provides humanitarian relief to Ukrainians affected by the war.

Voices of Children - provides food, water, hygiene kits, cash, and psychosocial support to children and families.

People In Need (select SOS Ukraine) - focuses on providing food, clean water, and hygienic products.

The National Bank of Ukraine ( for civilians ) - funds used to provide relief to civilians endangered by the war.
